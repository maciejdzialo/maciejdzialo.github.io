short summary

I’m a Data Analyst with a rich scientific background, passionate about turning data into meaningful stories that drive strategic decisions and positive change. With over eight years of experience in international research and two years in open-access scholarly publishing, I currently work as a Publishing Specialist at Frontiers Media. Here, I use my analytical expertise to support journal growth and engage with the global scientific community. My PhD in Life Sciences from Jagiellonian University has honed my skills in advanced statistical methods and interdisciplinary research.

Throughout my career, I’ve enjoyed designing experiments, ensuring research reproducibility, and crafting clear, data-driven narratives. My journey has taught me the importance of effective communication and problem-solving, always striving for the best solutions.

I’m driven by continuous learning and self-improvement, constantly seeking to enhance my skills and knowledge. I believe in the power of data to tell compelling stories and the value of simple, effective solutions. Embracing collaboration and innovation, I aim to help teams make smarter, data-driven decisions and contribute to a healthier planet.
